About Us

Tamara Kaye-Honey

Over two decades ago, Tamara founded House of Honey as a design studio to blur the lines between classic and contemporary, soulful and surprising - and she’s been redefining what luxury looks like ever since.
As Creative Director of the Los Angeles and Montecito studios, Tamara guides the vision of both public and private spaces, ensuring each space reflects the studio’s distinct point of view.
She oversees design direction, curating materials and furnishings, and collaborating closely with clients and the design team to bring each story to life with purpose and personality.
Tamara is a storyteller with an eye for elegance and a taste for the unconventional. Her bold point of view has been published internationally.

We create interiors that feel considered, personal, and enduring

Our perspective
